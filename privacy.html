Privacy Policy

At MyToolza, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our website and tools.

1. Information We Collect

- Personal Data: When you use forms (such as contact or feedback), we may collect your email address and other contact details.
- Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage information through cookies and third-party analytics to improve our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

- To respond to inquiries or support requests.
- To improve website functionality and user experience.
- To send important updates related to our services (only if you opt in).

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except: - When required by law or legal process. - To protect the rights and safety of MyToolza or its users.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to personalize content, analyze traffic, and improve user experience. You may disable cookies through your browser settings, though some features may not function correctly.

5. Data Retention

We retain collected information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described above. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You may contact us at any time to exercise these rights.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised date.

If you have questions or concerns about this policy, please contact us at support@mytoolza.com.
